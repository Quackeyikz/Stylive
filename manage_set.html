The Clothing Customizer Page

This page allows users to create and personalize clothing designs by selecting colors and styles. Users can preview their customized apparel in real-time and save their designs. Additionally, the page features a clothing selection carousel, enabling users to browse through their created outfits and share their favorite designs with others. With an intuitive interface and interactive elements, this tool makes it easy to visualize and showcase unique fashion choices.
